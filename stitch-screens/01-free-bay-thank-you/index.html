BEFORE YOU COME IN

What should I bring? expand_more
Please bring your full set of golf clubs and wear clean athletic shoes or spikeless golf shoes.
Can I bring a friend? expand_more
Your booking is for a private performance session. One additional guest is welcome to observe, but the bay time is optimized for a single golfer's data collection.
How accurate is the data? expand_more
We use industry-leading launch monitors accurate to within 0.1mph ball speed and 1-yard carry distance. This is the same technology used on the PGA Tour.
Is there a cancellation policy? expand_more
We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for any cancellations or rescheduling to allow other golfers to utilize the precision equipment.
Will a pro be assisting me? expand_more
A member of our technical staff will help you set up and ensure you're reading the data correctly, but the session is designed for your independent practice and analysis.
